About Eleri

Built by an operator,
not a slide deck.

Most software inside companies doesn't learn — it runs the same rules forever. Eleri's engines absorb every decision a team makes, and apply that judgment automatically, faster, every cycle after.

Founded and engineered solo from Ottawa, Canada — architecture, backend, and product design built in-house from day one. Accepted into Invest Ottawa's IO Flex program, with pilot companies already lined up for Elensi's launch.
