About Eleri

Built by an operator, not a slide deck.

Eleri Tech was founded to solve a simple problem: most software inside companies doesn't learn. It runs the same rules forever. Eleri's engines are designed to absorb every decision a team makes — and apply that judgment automatically, faster, every cycle after.

Founded and engineered solo from Ottawa, Canada — architecture, backend, and product design built in-house from day one. Accepted into Invest Ottawa's IO Flex program, with pilot companies already lined up for Elensi's launch.

The Core

One brain. It just wears different hats.

Every Eleri product runs on the same underlying learning core — the part that watches decisions, finds patterns, and improves the next recommendation.

1

Ingest

Raw, unfiltered data — resumes, trade logs, campaign data — goes in exactly as it exists, no pre-cleaning required.

2

Learn

The core watches what humans approve, reject, or change — and quietly adjusts its own weighting.

3

Apply

Recommendations, shortlists, and reports get sharper every cycle — without retraining or manual tuning.
